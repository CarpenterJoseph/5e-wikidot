Can we put the Races back on the main page?
ArcaediusX 03 Jan 2023 23:02

Please? Not only is it vastly more convenient to have the races listed individually and accessibly directly from the main page, instead of having to click and redirect to a 'category' page and then redirect to the race you want, but whoever decided they needed to revamp practically the entire race section back in April (according to history logs) also broke the 'Table of Contents' navigation - the links do not jump to those sections anymore and are off now. They also broke some of the races and some races are missing information (ie. Volo's Aasimar information, etc.)

Can we please just fix and revert that?

Re: Can we put the Races back on the main page?
Fredbob711 04 Jan 2023 16:19

Table of Contents stuff has been fixed on the main page. My apologies on that, I always forget to update that when I make changes on the main page to the number of headers out there.

I'm not sure what data is missing? The Volo's Aasimar is still there Aasimar. Is there some text missing that you're looking for? Both sets of pages existed for quite a while and I think we were pretty thorough with making sure we transferred all the data over. If you can point me to which races are broken or missing I'll definitely take a look.

The point of moving them off the main page was to reduce the clutter and it was getting harder and harder to keep the Races organized in that generic list. What if the ToC took you to the main Lineage page instead of that section of the main page?

Another driver of the changes was that the pages themselves had never been included in their own category on the site (ie dnd5e.wikidot.com/aasimar vs. dnd5e.wikidot.com/lineage:aasimar). There are several other sections on the main page that are in the same boat; namely backgrounds and feats. Lineages just ended up being the one tackled because of the category issue as well. I don't know if/when we'll do something to those other two to try and clean them up.

There was a discussion on the lineage page changes a while ago when we were planning them, nobody objected at the time. They sat out there for quite a while before we finally pushed them to the main page. Even had a test version of the home page for people to look at.
