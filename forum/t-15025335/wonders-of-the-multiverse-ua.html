Alright lets go, heres a starting batch to show progress:

http://dnd5e.wikidot.com/feat:cohort-of-chaos-ua

http://dnd5e.wikidot.com/feat:keenness-of-the-stone-giant-revised-ua

Alongside getting them up there, I've implemented a small change in how we usually format feats, in that, feats with prerequisite feats link to the feats they need. This is due to the fact that from information ive read, feats in this style are going to become a definite part of the game in future. This style of linking has been something I've seen or 3.5/pathfinder style websites before.

So yeah, thoughts on that? I think it makes the formatting better. If we like it, I think I'll go and update each other feat that uses prereq feats to link to their prereq. I might also do the same thing for background prerequisites too.

I also wonder if we should do the inverse as well? Another thing I picked up from reading 3.5/path srd's or whatnot, is that it might be a good idea to alongside saying "This is the prerequsite feat (link attachment)", we could also have a "This feat is a prerequisite for these feats", though thats something im more 50/50 in terms of it being a good idea, rather than properly in favour of like the prereq links. Maybe Ill create a test page to show test out and show what it looks like in a bit

In any case, ill be having the next set of feats up soon enough
